Nibble Baby is a food-tracking app created for parents and caregivers who want a simple, reliable way to support their baby’s introduction to solid foods. The app makes it easy to record and review what your baby has eaten, helping you keep track of new foods as well as those that may need to be avoided.

With features designed to monitor potential allergic reactions and sensitivities, Nibble Baby gives you peace of mind while navigating this important stage of development. The app also highlights patterns over time, allowing you to see whether your baby’s diet is balanced and varied, and giving you helpful insights at a glance.

By simplifying the process of tracking meals, Nibble Baby empowers parents and caregivers to feel confident about their baby’s nutrition. It’s designed to be accessible, easy to use, and a dependable companion during one of your child’s most exciting milestones — the journey into solid foods.
